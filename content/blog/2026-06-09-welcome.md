This is my study site! I'm currently working through **Genki I** in my college Japanese 2 class, and I'm using this site to track my progress, drill kanji, and post updates as I go.

Right now I'm on **Lesson 6** — the て-form is kicking my butt but I'm getting there. My Anki retention is sitting around 89% on mature cards, which I'm pretty happy with.

Things I want to remember:

- いく → いって is irregular, don't forget it
- きれい and きらい are な-adjectives even though they end in い (sneaky!)
- 好き takes が, not を
